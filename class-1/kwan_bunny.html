Bunnies are Cute

Safe Veggies for Consumption

  • Dandelion Greens
  • Carrot Greens (NOT the carrot)
  • Bok Choy Leaves
  • Arugula
  • Watercress
  • Cilantro and Basil
  • Yu Choy

Daily Diet should Consists of:

  • Unlimited Timothy Hay (include Alfafa Hay for Kits)
  • Fresh and clean "safe" veggies
  • Small slice of banana, apple (not the core), stawberry

Bunnies are great pets. However, like all things, preparation and research before getting one is key before making the journey!
